Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to expand the online market for Jianjun's local furniture store, offering a wide range of quality furniture and exceptional customer service.

Audience

This site targets customers looking for affordable, high-quality furniture. It serves individuals, families, and businesses in need of living room sets, bedroom sets, dining tables, and office furniture. The website is designed for easy navigation on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices, allowing customers to browse products, read reviews, and make purchases conveniently.
